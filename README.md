# #97 Double Space Killer | Fridge Combo

The ultimate digital hygiene tool for global professionals. It "kills" inconsistent spacing that can make your English writing look amateurish or messy. One tap to ensure perfect spacing hygiene.

## 核心 (Core Philosophy)
* **1 Tool / 1 Action**: Instantly fix spacing errors without complex settings or AI "over-editing."
* **Professional Standards**: Adheres to modern digital style guides (One space after periods, no double spacing).
* **Speed & Safety**: Designed for the final second before you hit "Send."

## 機能 (Features)
* **Kill Multi-Spaces (Primary Action)**: 
    * Collapses multiple spaces into a single space.
    * **Intelligent Space Insertion**: Automatically adds a missing space after `.`, `?`, and `!` if followed by a letter or number.
    * **Full-width Conversion**: Converts Japanese full-width spaces (`　`) into standard half-width spaces.
    * **Trailing Clean-up**: Removes leading/trailing spaces and cleans up excessive empty lines.
* **Tab to Space (Independent Action)**: Converts tab characters into spaces only when requested, preserving your document structure otherwise.
* **Maximum Viewer UI**: The interface is optimized to provide the largest possible text area on mobile devices, allowing you to see your edits in real-time.
